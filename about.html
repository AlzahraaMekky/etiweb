WHO WE ARE?

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT?

Efficiency Training Institute (eti), is an accredited professional training institute and management consultancy firm servicing a diversified clientele of working professionals and organizations throughout the region. eti is acknowledged for its innovative approach and high level of professional service. We provide a variety of organizational development courses, high management training programs, accounting and finance certifications, Procurement and supply chain a range of IT workshops.

Efficiency Training Institute is a Training and Consultancy Provider, comprises qualified and experienced trainers and facilitators who come from a variety of professional backgrounds and settings.Our team has been affected both personally and professionally by all of the aspects reflected within our training programs and consultancy services. We deliver our services to Government, Corporates and Community, and partner globally with individuals and organisations to assist them reaching their full potential and achieve success through:
  Providing Globally recognised certifications, qualifications and endorsing customised programs
  Developing innovative training Courses, programs, services and training materials and products
  Designing and implementing customized training programs and courses in various fields

Training Needs Analysis
When you choose ETI, you can select from our extensive range of 'off-the-shelf' courses, or ask for customised courses as per your specific requirements.

Whatever your specifications, we are here to ensure all your needs are met. We will take the time to understand your organisation’s needs and offer proper training solutions that satisfy your business objectives. Working together, we will facilitate the development of your people and improve your processes and systems. Whichever your option might be, rest assured that you will be trained by a high-profile expert.

Make our experience your expertise with our leading professional trainers.

Our Trainers are not just qualified, but experts in their fields and professionally recognised, capable off delivering you a first class learning experience.

They are trained to understand and meet your different learning needs, and have years of industry experience. Moreover, our specialists will closely work with your organisation to keep your learning in line with your organisation’s objectives.

Business success starts with people; we understand that. Hence, we have conducted numerous Training Needs Analysis to aid in achieving optimum performance.

We also train on both Corporate and Individual levels; from beginners to experts, thus, expedite the progress of your people with cutting edge training techniques and methodologies. Whatever your requirements are, we are ready.
