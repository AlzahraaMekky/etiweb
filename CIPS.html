Chartered Institute of Procurement and Supply (CIPS)

The Chartered Institute of Procurement & Supply (CIPS) is the premier global organisation serving the procurement and supply chain profession. Dedicated to promoting best practice, CIPS provides a wide range of courses which enable people working in Procurement to gain globally recognised professional qualifications. As an accredited CIPS provider, Efficiency Training Institute is the CIPS approved training provider for Sultanate of Oman. We are offering CIPS courses to enable organisations to benefit from our tutors with international procurement experience and enhance the skills of their Procurement functions both locally and globally. Qualified procurement professionals can add more value to the bottom line by using a range of techniques to improve cost, quality & delivery, and overall supply chain performance.

Why Choose eti?

  1. Courses are delivered by MCIPS qualified procurement professionals with global industry experience
  2. Our courses are loaded with industry examples to enable clients to understand how the learning is applied
  3. Our case studies are based on projects we have delivered and not just on academic theory
  4. Most of our tutor team are CIPS examiners and offer Exam Technique Coaching based on their experience
  5. Our exam pass rates are above the average
  6. We are Procurement Specialists and CIPS is our core offering
  7. We offer flexible formats – Morning, Afternoon and Evening.

About CIPS Qualification

Throughout the world, CIPS qualifications are recognised as driving leading edge thinking and professionalism in the profession. Each of its qualifications has been developed by working closely with our industry partners to make sure they reflect current, emerging and best practice in procurement and supply chain management. CIPS offers five qualifications to support your professional development in procurement and supply. On successful completion of the Diploma, Advanced and Professional diplomas in conjunction with three years experience in a role of responsibility in procurement and supply.
